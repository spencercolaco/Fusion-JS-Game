Welcome to Fusion - the game.

You are a hydrogen (H) atom. Fuse with other H atoms to create larger elements. Once you reach iron (Fe) your star will go super nova and you win the game. Control your atom with the arrow keys. Protip: Beware of antimatter! Most important: relax.

* Press start to play again.
